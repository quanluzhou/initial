About me

Hi! I am a graduate student at UC Berkeley focusing on 2D&3D design(architectural, UI/UX, graphic...).

A fan and self-learner of design, art, and music. I got to know graphic design around 2010 and Photoshop has been my friend ever since. A detail-oriented person who uses interdisciplinary approaches to solve user-centered problems. I majored in Chemistry and Architecture, and have been learning design software and doing creative designs in my spare time.

I believe a great user-centered design needs to be both organized and creative. As a designer with a scientific background, I am good at doing research and analysis to track data changes; as an art-lover for over 10 years, I am good at using various tools (2d/3d, physical/digital, traditional/innovative.. ....) to create prototypes and present design works. I think there are no boundaries between disciplines and the most innovative solutions always come from the collision of multiple ideas. Science, nature, art, and humanities... everything I am passionate about can be a source of inspiration for my designs. My experience and knowledge allow me to often think of innovative solutions to challenges and am always ready to add new knowledge and tools to my skill set. If your team is looking for a designer who is creative, unafraid of challenges, and has an eye for detail, I would be the perfect match.
